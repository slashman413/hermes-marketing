# Bluesky profile seeding kit

The auto-poster is live (2 promo posts/day). But promo lands badly on a cold,
empty profile — Bluesky rewards accounts that look human and participate.
Spend ~15 minutes once on the below so every automated post arrives on a real
profile. Copy-paste ready.

## 1. Profile basics (Settings → Edit Profile)

**Display name:** `slashman · AI 工具人`

**Bio (under 256 chars):**
```
Solo dev building in public. 20+ free browser tools, a Taiwan-stock signal scanner, and automation that runs on GitHub Actions while I sleep. Everything open source.
🔗 slashmantools.us
```

**Avatar/banner:** reuse `og.png` from the hub, or a simple 🤖 on brand color
(#0ea5e9). Even a plain avatar beats the default egg — the default screams bot.

## 4. Ongoing (optional, high-leverage)

- Reply genuinely to 2–3 posts in #buildinpublic / #indiedev each day. Replies
  build reach far faster than broadcasting.
- The auto-poster handles promo; your job is the human layer — a few replies
  and the occasional non-promo thought. 5 min/day.

## Why this matters

A brand-new account whose entire history is 2 promo links/day gets throttled and
ignored — on every platform. 15 minutes of seeding + a few minutes of daily
engagement is what turns the (already-built) automation into actual reach.
The infra is done; this is the part only you can do.
